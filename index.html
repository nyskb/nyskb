The purpose of this project is to highlight all the train lines in the NYC MTA Subway. We want to show people adventures they can take on each train line. This project was created because there is so much in New York City that people do not know about.


Welcome to NYC

With every swipe, you end up in a different world.

Coney Island

Take the D, F, N, or Q to Coney Island-Stillwell Avenue to enjoy fun rides and the beautiful beach.

The Apollo Theater

Take the A, B, C, or D to 125th Street to enjoy the Jazz culture.

Yankee Stadium

Take the 4, B, or D to 161st Street-Yankee Stadium to support your favorite baseball team.

Columbus Circle

Take the A, B, C, D to Columbus Circle to enjoy great shopping and food.

Times Square

Take the 1, 2, 3, 7, A, C, E, N, Q, or R to Times Square and be one of the million people who congregrate to celebrate the 'Dropping of the Ball' on New Year's Eve.

Bryant Park

Take the B, D, F, M, or 7 to Bryant Park. Did you know it used to be a Cemetery for NYC's Poor?

Citi Field

Take the 7 train to Mets-Willets Point. It's home to the New York Mets!

Barclays Center

Take the 2, 3, 4, 5, B, D, N, Q, or R to Atlantic Ave-Barclays Center, to the highest-grossing music venue in the United States.

Prospect Park

Take F, G, B, or Q to Prospect Park to enjoy the 585 acre, green heart of Brooklyn.

Rockefeller Centre

Take the B,D,F,M to 47th-50th Streets-Rockefeller Center to show off your skills at the ice rink!
